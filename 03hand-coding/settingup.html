Setting up the Gun and the Canvas

How to Get Started

Setting up a tufting gun properly is crucial for a successful tufting project. Here are the general steps to get your tufting gun ready for use:

  1. Load The Tufting Gun:
    You may need to refer to the manufacturer's instruction manual to load the gun.

We use the Tuft the World Cut Pile Gun. Here is a video on how to string this specific machine.

  1. Adjust the Needle Depth:
    Most tufting guns allow you to adjust the depth at which the needle penetrates the backing material. Start with the recommended setting for your yarn and backing material, and make adjustments as needed for your project.
  2. Begin Tufting and make sure you maintain consistancy.
