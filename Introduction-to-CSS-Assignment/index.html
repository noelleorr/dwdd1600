Rug Tufting

What is Rug Tufting?

Rug tufting is a versatile and creative textile art form that involves the creation of rugs and tapestries by looping or tufting yarn through a backing material, typically canvas or burlap.
Rug tufting not only results in functional and decorative floor coverings but also serves as a unique form of artistic expression, making it a captivating and enduring craft.
