FAQ

What size should my rug frame be?

Keep in mind that larger rugs may require larger frames or even specialized stands or frames designed for oversized rug tufting projects. The choice of frame size should be based on the size of the rug you plan to make.
What is a cut pile tufting gun?
Cut pile tufting guns are designed to create a pile where the tufted yarn is cut at the top, resulting in individual, evenly cut yarn loops that create a plush and soft texture on the surface.
What is a loop pile tufting gun?
Loop pile tufting guns, on the other hand, create loops of yarn that are not cut at the top. The loops remain intact, creating a textured surface with loops of varying heights, which can give a more casual or rugged appearance.
